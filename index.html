About me…

I'm Prajwalit Bhopale. A software engineer from Pune. I did my engineering from Pune Institute of Computer Technology. Currently working as a 'Front-End Engineer' for a startup called Infinitely Beta.

Apart from all that, I'm a novice photographer, a guitarist (well, sort of…), a movie/music buff and a books lover. Team Kindle!
